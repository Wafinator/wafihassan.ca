About Me

I'm a Computer Science student at the University of Windsor, currently pursuing my Bachelor of Science degree with an expected graduation in December 2025. My academic journey has been marked by excellence, earning a place on the Faculty of Science Dean's Honour Roll in 2021.

I've gained valuable industry experience as a Software Tester Intern at Philips, where I worked with the Ultrasound Reacts SQA team. During my two terms, I expanded from manual testing to automation QA, setting up local testing environments and developing automated test scripts using C# and xUnit. I collaborated closely with developers and product managers, executing test cases in TestRail and managing bug tracking through Jira to ensure high-quality software releases.

My technical expertise spans multiple programming languages including Java, Python, C#, and JavaScript, along with frameworks like React.js and Node.js. I'm passionate about building innovative solutions, from machine learning applications to full-stack web development. I approach every project with attention to detail, a focus on user experience, and a commitment to writing clean, maintainable code.

When I'm not coding or studying, I enjoy exploring new technologies, working on personal projects, and staying current with the latest industry trends. I'm always eager to take on new challenges and contribute to meaningful software solutions.
